Ten thousand years before the start of the game, Hyrule is an advanced civilization that built four huge animal-like machines called divine beasts and a legion of guardians to ward off Ganon. When Ganon returned, he was defeated by a princess with the help of the four champions and a knight.

The Champions' Ballad introduces a new Main Quest, as well as several Side Quests to obtain new Items. After freeing the four Divine Beasts and speaking to the four leaders of each respective tribe, Link is telepathically contacted by Zelda who tells him to return to the Shrine of Resurrection on the Great Plateau. After inserting the Sheikah Slate in the pedestal there, a Monk known as Maz Koshia speaks to him. He promises Link that if he can prove his worth, he may tame a Divine Beast of his own.
